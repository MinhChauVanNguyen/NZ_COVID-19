Key definitions:

⋆⋆ Priotised Ethnicity - “each person being allocated to a SINGLE ethnic group based on the ethnic groups they have identified with”. The order is followed by Māori, Pacific, Asian and Other.

⋆⋆ NZ Deprivation Index - describes New Zealand’s least to most deprived areas. A value of 10 on the index of deprivation indicates that the area is in the most deprived 10 percent of areas in New Zealand.

⋆⋆ Population Estimate - this is based on 2019 Census subnational population estimates.

Limitations of data analysis:

⋆⋆ The data excludes those that replied “Unknown” for Ethnicity (n = 1656 observations) hence doesn’t take these individuals into account.

⋆⋆ The date for number of tests taken is unavailable
